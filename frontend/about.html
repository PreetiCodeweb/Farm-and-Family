Why we started

A family's promise, grown into a movement.

Farm & Family began with one simple frustration: the vegetables in the city looked nothing like the ones grown back home, and the farmers who grew the real thing were never paid fairly for it.

We set out to close that gap — not with another marketplace app, but with a direct line between the people who grow food and the people who eat it. Every farm we work with is visited, verified, and given a fair, transparent price. Every product carries a Harvest Passport so you always know exactly where your food came from.

Today we work with farmers across Maharashtra, Karnataka, Punjab, and Kerala — and we're growing, one village at a time.
